Our Story

Built for Modern E-Commerce Teams

The E-Commerce Admin Portal was created to solve a common challenge: managing sales data, product catalogs, and business performance across multiple channels shouldn't require a dozen different tools.

Our platform brings together real-time analytics, inventory management, and team performance tracking into a single, beautifully designed interface. Every chart, every table, every number is crafted to give you clarity — not complexity.

Built with modern web technologies, our portal works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile — so you can monitor your business from anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you need? Use the form above.

How do I get started with the dashboard?

Simply navigate to the Sign In page, create an account with your email, and you'll have full access to the analytics dashboard, product catalog, and all other features.

Is the portal free to use?

Yes, the current version with all dashboard tabs, product catalog, and analytics is completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future.

Can I use social login (Google, GitHub, Facebook)?

Absolutely. On the Sign In page, you can authenticate using your Google, GitHub, or Facebook account for quick and secure access.

How is the sales data updated?

The dashboard currently uses mock data to demonstrate all features. In a production environment, it would connect to your e-commerce platform's API for real-time data.

Do you offer custom development services?

Yes! We offer custom e-commerce portal development, dashboard design, and data visualization services. Reach out via the contact form with your project details.

Is the portal mobile responsive?

Yes, every page is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. The product catalog, dashboard, and all forms adapt to any screen size.
